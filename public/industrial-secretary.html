Letter templates

Each year group uses a separate Google Doc template. To get the URL: open the Google Doc from your Drive, then copy the URL from your browser's address bar — it starts with https://docs.google.com/document/d/…. Save the URL, then click Open in Google Docs to edit it. Changes you make in the Doc apply to every approved letter automatically.
